# Product Overview

An AI-powered agent that assists job seekers by analyzing job descriptions against their resume, identifying skill gaps, rewriting cover letters, and suggesting areas for professional development.

## Core Capabilities

- **Resume-to-Job Matching**: Parse a job description and compare requirements against the user's resume to surface alignment and gaps
- **Cover Letter Generation**: Produce tailored cover letters that highlight relevant experience for each position
- **Gap Analysis & Learning Suggestions**: Identify missing skills or qualifications and recommend concrete steps to close them
- **Conversational Agent Interface**: Provide an interactive Blazor-based UI where users can upload documents and iterate on results

## Target Use Cases

- A developer preparing to apply for a specific role and wanting a targeted cover letter
- A professional exploring new career directions who needs to understand skill gaps
- Anyone seeking data-driven feedback on how well their resume matches a job posting

## Value Proposition

Automates the tedious, error-prone work of tailoring application materials for each position, giving candidates faster turnaround and higher-quality, personalized results.
